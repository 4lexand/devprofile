PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Sentinel Forensic ("we", "our", or "the App") is designed with a "Privacy First" architecture. We do not operate cloud servers to store your photos or personal data.

2. Data Collection & Usage

  • Photos: All photos captured are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your raw photos to any server.
  • Location Data (GPS): The App accesses your location strictly to embed coordinates into the photo metadata and generate the forensic hash. This data never leaves your device except as part of the hashed "fingerprint" you choose to publish.
  • Blockchain Data: When you choose to certify evidence, a SHA-256 hash (a digital fingerprint) is sent to the Solana Blockchain. This hash cannot be reversed to reveal the original photo or location.

3. Third-Party Services

The App interacts with:

  • Solana Blockchain: Public ledger for certification.
  • Arweave (Optional): If you choose to upload assets.

4. User Rights

Since we do not store your data, we cannot delete it for you. You have full control to delete the App and its local data from your device at any time.
