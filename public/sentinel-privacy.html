SENTINEL FORENSIC

Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Sentinel Forensic ("the App") is a decentralized tool designed to certify the existence and integrity of digital files on the Solana Blockchain. We prioritize user privacy and data sovereignty.

2. Data Collection & Storage

We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data.

The App operates entirely locally on your device (Client-Side). Your photos, GPS location, and metadata never leave your phone unless you explicitly choose to share the generated file yourself.

3. Device Permissions

  • Camera: Used solely to capture evidence photos in real-time. No background recording occurs.
  • Location (GPS): Used solely to imprint exact coordinates onto the evidence image for forensic validation. This data is not tracked or sent to our servers.
  • Internet: Used solely to communicate with the Solana Blockchain RPC nodes via your connected Wallet.

4. Blockchain Interaction & Fees

When you choose to "Certify On-Chain", the App constructs a transaction. By signing this transaction, you acknowledge:

  • Public Ledger: The SHA-256 hash of your file will be permanently recorded on the Solana public blockchain. This action is irreversible.
  • No File Upload: The image itself is NOT uploaded to the blockchain, only its cryptographic fingerprint (Hash).
  • Service Fee: Each certification transaction includes a fixed fee of 0.0009 SOL sent to the Sentinel Treasury to support ongoing development and maintenance of the Sentinel ecosystem. This is in addition to the standard Solana network fee (gas).
