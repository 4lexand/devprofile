HOW SENTINEL WORKS

Sentinel Forensic is a tool designed to capture Immutable Truth. This guide explains how we protect your privacy while proving the authenticity of your evidence.

1. Privacy Architecture (Local Storage)

The most important feature of Sentinel is privacy. When you capture evidence:

  • The Full Photo remains 100% LOCAL on your device.
  • We DO NOT upload your photos to any cloud, server, or database.
  • You maintain full custody of the original file within your device's secure storage.

2. What goes on the Blockchain?

If the photo stays on the phone, how do we certify it? We use Cryptography.

When you sign the transaction, we generate a Digital Fingerprint (SHA-256 Hash). This fingerprint is permanently written into the MEMO field of the Solana transaction.

Analogy: The "Memo" is like the notes section of a bank transfer, but immutable and public forever.

3. The "Tamper-Proof" Verification

This is the core security feature. The App calculates the ID (Hash) of the photo in your gallery in real-time and compares it to the ID recorded on the Blockchain.

⚠️ CRITICAL WARNING:
If you edit the photo in your gallery (crop, filter, adjust color) or modify even 1 pixel:

1. The Local ID (Hash) will change immediately.
2. It will NOT MATCH the Hash stored in the Blockchain Memo.
3. The evidence will be flagged as "TAMPERED" / INVALID.

4. How to Verify (Step-by-Step)

After a successful capture, you can verify your evidence independently:

1 Go to History in the App and tap the "Solana Explorer" link.
2 On the Explorer page, scroll down to the section labeled "Instruction" or "Memo".
3 You will see a text string starting with "Sentinel Proof: [Your-Hash]".
4 Compare that text with the Hash shown in your App. If they match, the photo is 100% authentic and existed at the timestamp shown on the block.
